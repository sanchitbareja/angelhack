Creating a Piece of Toast

Making a new piece of Toast is as simple as uploading a text file. As long as you follow a few simple rules, our website will take care of everything for you. Any text file should be fine!

The Title Delimiter

Every new slide in your Toast presentation starts off with a Title A Title is delimited on both sides with the pound or hashtag symbol. Under a title is any text that will go on that slide.
